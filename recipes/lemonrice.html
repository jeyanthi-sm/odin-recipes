Lemon Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 lime
  • Green chillies 2
  • 25gms of roased peanuts or roased Cashews
  • pinch of turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1tsp of Mustard Seeds
  • 1 tbsp of cooking oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Squeeze the lime and Remove the seeds
  2. Add salt to taste in the juice
  3. Heat a frying pan
  4. Add oil or ghee to it
  5. When it is hot add the mustard seeds. Wait till it seasoned well
  6. Add peanut to it
  7. Add turmeric powder to it
  8. Switch off the flase
  9. Now Add the extracted lime juice
  10. finally add the rice. Mix well.
